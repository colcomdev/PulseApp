### Quick Setup Options

**1. Gemma 4 (Free, Private, Local, Multimodal)**
Google's latest open model — text, image & audio. Runs locally via Ollama.
- Click **Switch** in the header and select **Gemma 4** for guided setup
- Or manually: Download Ollama from https://ollama.com/download/windows, then run: `ollama pull gemma4:e4b`

**2. Ollama (Free, Private, Local)**
Your code never leaves your machine.
- Download Ollama: https://ollama.com/download/windows
- Then run: `ollama pull qwen2.5-coder:7b`
- Evolve AI will detect it automatically

**3. Cloud AI (API key required)**
- **Anthropic Claude**: https://console.anthropic.com/
- **OpenAI**: https://platform.openai.com/api-keys
- **HuggingFace**: https://huggingface.co/settings/tokens

Click **Switch** in the header to configure any provider.

💡 **Windows tip:** If Ollama is installed but not detected, change `aiForge.ollamaHost` to `http://127.0.0.1:11434` in Settings.
